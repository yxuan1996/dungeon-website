ABOUT US

From a humble beginning the day his kitchen was born, Sng is known for his seemlingly effortless yet delicious cooking.

Due to his outstanding contributions in the field of culinary arts, his restaurant, The Dungeon, has been awarded 5 Michelin Stars. He has also been featured in numerous publications such as The New York Times and BBC News.

Come and discover the legendary Dungeon Dining Experience for yourself!
